Our Story

Dining Inside Living History

Carriages isn't just a place to eat — it's an experience. Tucked within Garden Park Tickenham, our beautifully restored vintage railway carriages offer a one-of-a-kind setting that transports you to another era the moment you step aboard.

Whether you're settling in for a leisurely breakfast, a proper Sunday roast, or a pot of tea and homemade cake, you'll find us warm, welcoming, and worth every mile of the journey.
